    SPACE MISSION ENGINEERING



    a.i. solutions has been delivering full lifecycle space mission engineering and technical services including satellite operations, flight dynamics analysis, sustaining engineering, ground system development, program management, facilities management, and mission-critical IT support for both civilian (NASA, NOAA) and defense (US Air Force, NRO) space agencies. With more than 300 successfully supported missions, our top priority remains ensuring your mission’s success.

    MISSILE DEFENSE SYSTEMS ASSURANCE



    a.i. solutions provides functional and subject matter expertise in engineering, technology, processes, production, and programmatic services related to mission critical and high reliability systems. We support the Missile Defense Agency (MDA), US Army Space and Missile Defense Command (SMDC), and NASA’s Marshall Space Flight Center (MSFC) in all phases of defense acquisition and the development and operation of critical space and missile defense systems. a.i. solutions’ quality and mission assurance solutions focus on product and process compliance, which prevent mistakes or defects in the as designed systems. The goal is to meet mission objectives and avoid non-conformances in the solutions or services that provide capabilities to the warfighter.
